Welcome, I am Insuk Cho.

Insuk Cho is a highly seasoned Software Engineer with over 23 years of experience spanning South Korea and the United States. He currently serves as a Principal Support Engineer & foraml Solution Tech Lead at @Elastic. His background primarily includes B2B enterprise projects in finance, manufacturing and power plants, utilizing core technologies like Java & Spring framework & Python. He possesses a deep interest and experience in machine learning algorithms for prediction future abnormal behavior for timeseries data. Insuk is also an accomplished Agile/Lean evangelist and has previously served as a development lead in Agile environments, promotingin Scrum, TDD, pair programming, CI/CD, lean product development within cross-functional team.

Driven by a passion for knowledge sharing, Insuk has authored five books—covering Python for novice programmers and Node.js for practical business application — translated three books, and supervised two others. He volunteers as a mentor at Changbal (창발) Society, a nonprofit organization dedicated to helping Koreans currently working in the tech industry within the greater Seattle area. He is also committed to writing insightful articles to promote a better software development culture in South Korea. Interested readers can find his work on his blog and his YouTube channel.

He has a proven track record of successful collaboration with top global engineers at organization like @ Pivotal Labs in San Francisco, where he served as both a Product Manager and Developer. He has successfully partnered with teams across Germany, the U.K., India, and other nations. Insuk is currently seeking opportunities to collaborate with highly seasoned global professionals as a Principal Software Engineer, leveraging his extensive, real-world Agile experience to drive impactful business outcomes and continuous learning.
